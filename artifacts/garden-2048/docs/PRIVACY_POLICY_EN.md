# Garden 2048 Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 15, 2026**
**Effective Date: April 15, 2026**

Sprout Lab Garden ("Company", "we", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile game **Garden 2048** ("Service"), in accordance with Google Play Developer Program Policies, GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1-1. Automatically Collected Information
While you use the Service, the following information may be collected automatically:

| Category | Items | Collected By |
|----------|-------|--------------|
| Device | Device model, OS version, language, screen size | Company / Google |
| Advertising Identifier | Android Advertising ID (AAID) | Google AdMob |
| Network | IP address (approximate location), carrier | Google AdMob |
| Usage | Session length, ad impressions/clicks | Google AdMob |

### 1-2. Locally Stored Data (Not Transmitted)
The following gameplay data is stored only in your device's `localStorage` and is not transmitted to our servers or third parties:

- Game progress (cleared stages, high scores, coins, inventory)
- Card collection, daily/weekly mission progress
- Theme, language, and sound settings
- Subscription state flags (e.g. trial usage)

### 1-3. Payment Information
Subscription payments are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not directly collect or store payment methods (such as card numbers); we only receive transaction results (order ID, subscription status) from Google.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for:

1. Providing the Service and saving/restoring game progress
2. Displaying personalized ads (AdMob)
3. Developing new content, statistical analysis, and quality improvement
4. Preventing abuse and enhancing security
5. Compliance with legal obligations

We do not use information for purposes beyond the above without your consent.

---

## 3. Data Retention

- **Locally stored data**: Retained on your device until you uninstall the app, then permanently deleted.
- **Automatically collected information (advertising IDs, etc.)**: Retained per Google AdMob's policy (typically up to 14 months).
- **Payment data**: Retained by Google Play for 5 years per applicable consumer protection law.

---

## 4. Sharing and Processors

We do not sell or provide your personal data to third parties for their independent use. The following SDKs/platforms process data on our behalf:

| Processor | Purpose | Data | Policy |
|-----------|---------|------|--------|
| Google LLC (AdMob) | Ad serving and measurement | Ad ID, device/network info | https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads |
| Google LLC (Play Billing) | Subscription payments | Payment, transaction info | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google LLC (Play Services) | Authentication, updates | Device identifiers | https://policies.google.com/privacy |

---

## 5. Advertising ID and Personalized Ads

- The Service uses Google AdMob to display ads.
- On Android 14+, you can reset or delete your Advertising ID under Settings > Privacy > Ads.
- For users identified as under 13, we configure AdMob to serve only Non-Personalized Ads (NPA).

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

- The Service is intended for users **aged 13 and older**.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents/guardians who become aware that their child has provided personal information may request its deletion.
- The Service complies with Google Play Families Policy.

## 7. Your Rights

You (or your legal representative) may at any time:

1. Request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing
2. Reset or delete your Advertising ID via device settings
3. Cancel your subscription via Google Play > Subscriptions
4. Delete the app to remove all locally stored data

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We will respond promptly.
